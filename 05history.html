HISTORY





Blue Canvas

Julian Shaw


This track captures the profound silence reflected on a window pane just before dawn, amidst the muffled complexity of urban life. Its minimalist structure and patient tempo guide the listener into a meditative state, achieving a rare tranquility. The sparse arrangement emphasizes the delicate space between notes, making silence an integral part of the melody itself.

Smoke & Standards

Julian Shaw


A collection of timeless standards performed as raw, unaccompanied piano solos, recorded in the intimate setting of a dimly lit, historic jazz club. The music feels like the lingering scent of cigar smoke—nostalgic, warm, and deeply personal. It strips away all artifice and virtuosity, leaving only the pure, unadorned emotional core of each classic melody.
